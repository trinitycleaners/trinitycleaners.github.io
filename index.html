From one surface to the next

At Trinity Cleaners we strongly believe in protecting the environment, along with individuals coming into contact with our recently serviced areas. That being said, it is important to us to ensure that our products will sanitize any surface safely to the human touch, while also remaining environmentally efficient by using green certified products.

What we provide

At Trinity Cleaners we believe in providing the proper equipment necessary to maintain appropriate housekeeping. This means we will not store any equipment at your facility, the only evidence of our existence will be cleanliness.

Trinity Cleaners will provide the following:
  • Cleaning Supplies
  • Vacuum Cleaners
  • Mops/Brooms
  • Dusting Equipment
  • Waste Basket Liners

At an additional cost Trinity Cleaners will provide Hand Soap, Hand Towels. If not specified, client will be responsible for Hand Soap, Hand Towels, and Toilet Paper.

Housekeeping Standards

When it comes to cleanliness we believe there is no compromising! Your facility will receive our absolute thorough service for each and every scheduled visit guaranteed.

  • Waste baskets will be emptied and disinfected daily to eliminate odorous bacteria. The collected waste will be relocated to the appropriate waste receptacle.
  • Dust removal from furniture, windows, tables, filing cabinets and all other surfaces requiring dust removal including office equipment.
  • Drinking water fountain(s) cleanliness will be maintained.
  • Light switches, door knobs, cabinet handles, and all common areas subject to frequent traffic will be disinfected regularly.
  • Dining area including exterior of cabinets, exterior of refrigerator, and exterior of microwave, counter tops and floor will be maintained and sanitized appropriately.
  • Bathrooms will be cleaned and sanitized including toilets, urinals, sinks and floors.
